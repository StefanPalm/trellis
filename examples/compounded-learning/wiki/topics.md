# Topic charter

The dimensions along which this wiki accumulates knowledge, proposed at
creation and maintained by agents. Changes are staged as proposals in
[log.md](/log.md).

## practices

The purpose is about what works: concrete habits, cadences, and workflows are
the primary dimension. Currently the largest topic (3 pages), as expected.

## failure-modes

A practice is understood by how it dies. Documented failure modes are what
make the practice pages trustworthy rather than aspirational.

## foundations

The patterns and formats this practice builds on — the llm-wiki idea, OKF,
agent skills — will keep generating material as they evolve.

## open-questions

The purpose is open-ended, so the wiki tracks its own frontier. Fed by lint;
empty so far.
